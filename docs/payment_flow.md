# Klino Payment Flow

## Goal

Create a secure payment process that protects customers, agents, and Klino.

---

# Step 1: Customer Booking

Customer:

- Selects cleaning service
- Chooses date and time
- Provides address
- Reviews price estimate

Then:

Customer confirms booking.

---

# Step 2: Payment

Customer makes payment.

Payment status:

- Pending

After successful payment:

Payment status:

- Paid

Money is held securely.

---

# Step 3: Escrow Holding

Payment is temporarily held.

Status:

- In Escrow

The money is not immediately released to the agent.

---

# Step 4: Agent Completes Job

Agent:

- Accepts booking
- Arrives at location
- Uploads before photos
- Completes cleaning
- Uploads after photos
- Marks job complete

---

# Step 5: Customer Confirmation

Customer reviews:

- Before photos
- After photos
- Completed service

Customer chooses:

Confirm Completion

or

Report Issue

---

# Step 6A: Successful Completion

If customer confirms:

Payment status:

Released

Money goes to Klino.

Klino pays agent.

---

# Step 6B: Dispute

If customer reports a problem:

Payment status:

On Hold

Admin reviews:

- Photos
- Booking details
- Customer complaint
- Agent response

Admin decides:

- Release payment
- Partial refund
- Full refund

---

# Agent Earnings

Agent dashboard displays:

- Completed jobs
- Pending earnings
- Available earnings
- Payment history

---
